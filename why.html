Why PCS?

Pitches and Routes When Making Your Calls

What is PCS?
PCS (Professional Computing Services) and is an All-In-One transportation-management-system (TMS) that helps with dispatch, accounting, and fleet management on one platform.
Standard Voicemail
Good Morning [Prospect], this is [Me] with PCS Software. I'm calling to see if you and your company are in the market for a transportation-management-system.
Prospect Answers

Hi [Prospect], this is [Me] with PCS, how are you doing today?

*Response*

That's great! I don't want to waste too much of your time, but I was checking to see if you are in the market for a transportation-management-system?

*Response* - No - Okay, thank you for your time.

*Response* - Yes/Wants to hear more about it.

Fantastic! Well PCS is an all-in-one transportation-management-system that combines dispatch, accounting, and fleet management in one place. May I take 5 minutes of your time for some qualifying questions?

*Ask Qualifying Questions* or *Schedule For Another Call-Back*
