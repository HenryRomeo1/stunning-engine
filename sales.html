Common Questions and Responses

What is PCS?
PCS (Platform Connecting Services) and is an All-In-One transportation-management-system (TMS) that helps with dispatch, accounting, and fleet management on one platform.
Do you work with shippers or carriers
We are the only TMS software that provides services to shippers, carriers, and brokerages in one powerful location.
Can drivers use the software to upload BOLs?
Yes, they can easily use it and be up to date with them.
Are you a load boarding or 3PL company?
No...
How long is Implementation?
We take half the time to implement compared to other TMS platforms. We implement in 2 quarters while other will take 4-5 quarters.
How much does this cost?
We sell 3 packages that range from Silver, Gold, and Diamond. Further information on pricing will be discussed with the Sales Rep. (Note that the packages are customizable)
Can you work on my phone?
We operate on both desktop and mobile phones.
